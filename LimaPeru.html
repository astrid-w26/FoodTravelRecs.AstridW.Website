Lima

Food in Lima, Peru:

Breakfast

Breakfast at the Perroquet Restaurant inside Country Club Lima Hotel was an excellent way to start the day. The breakfast buffet had a great variety of fresh pastries, fruit, eggs, and traditional Peruvian options, all beautifully presented. The service was attentive and welcoming, and the elegant atmosphere made the experience feel relaxing and upscale. I especially appreciated the quality of the coffee and the peaceful setting overlooking the hotel’s classic interior.

Lunch

Lunch at Osaka was one of the highlights of my trip to Lima. The menu offered an incredible mix of Japanese and Peruvian flavors, with every dish tasting fresh, creative, and perfectly balanced. The service was polished and attentive, and the modern atmosphere made it feel lively without being overwhelming. I’d highly recommend trying the sushi and signature seafood dishes if you visit for lunch.

Dinner

Dining at Astrid y Gastón was an unforgettable experience and easily one of the best meals I had in Lima. Every course was beautifully presented and full of unique flavors that showcased Peruvian cuisine in a creative and refined way. The service was exceptional from start to finish, and the elegant setting made the entire evening feel special. I would highly recommend it for anyone looking for a memorable fine dining experience.
